What is the AP Poverty Clock?

A real-time visualization of Andhra Pradesh's poverty elimination mission, built for policymakers, researchers, and citizens.

⏱️

Real-Time Poverty Clock

Watch the poverty count tick downward every second, computed from NFHS survey data and demographic projections.

🗺️

Interactive District Map

Explore all 26 districts with color-coded HCR data, population stats, and year-by-year poverty trends.

📊

District Rankings

Sort districts by poverty count, HCR, or population. Track progress with live progress bars toward zero.

📍

670+ Mandal Maps

Drill down into every district to see mandal-level boundaries, codes, and administrative detail.

📅

2015–2029 Timeline

Scrub through every year from baseline (NFHS-4) to target (2029) and watch data change in real-time.

📱

Mobile Responsive

Fully optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops. Touch-friendly maps with adaptive layouts.

About the Organization

Global Forum for Sustainable Transformation

GFST is a pioneering organization dedicated to the Next Era of Governance. We work at the intersection of technology, policy, and data to build tools that empower transparent, accountable, and forward-looking governance systems across India and beyond.

The AP Poverty Clock is one of GFST's flagship initiatives — making poverty data accessible, real-time, and visual for every citizen, policymaker, and researcher.
